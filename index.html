My Vision for Gem

As your representative, I am committed to bringing positive change to our
community through these key priority areas.

✔️ Economic Empowerment

Creating sustainable job opportunities and supporting local businesses in Gem Constituency.

✔️ Quality Education

Ensuring all students have access to quality education and scholarship opportunities.

✔️ Infrastucture Development

Developing roads, water systems and electricity access across all villages.

✔️ Youth Empowerment

Creating opportunities for youth through skills training and entrepreneurship programs.

✔️ Agriculture Innovation

Supporting farmers with modern farming techniques and market access.

✔️ healthcare Access

Improving healthcare facilities and ensuring affordable healthcare for all residents.
