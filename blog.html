Campaign Updates

Stay informed about our latest news, events, and initiatives as we
work together to build a better Gem Constituency.

Economic Empowerment

Creating sustainable job opportunities through investment in agriculture, small businesses, and skills development programs to reduce unemployment and poverty in our constituency.

Quality Education

Ensuring all children in Gem have access to quality education by improving school infrastructure, providing learning materials, and implementing scholarship programs for deserving students.

Infrastucture Development

Developing critical infrastructure including roads, water systems, and electricity access to improve the quality of life and support economic activities across all parts of Gem Constituency..

Youth Empowerment

Creating opportunities for youth through mentorship, skills training, sports programs, and entrepreneurship support to help them build successful futures and contribute to community development.

Agriculture Innovation

Supporting farmers with modern farming techniques, access to markets, irrigation systems, and agricultural inputs to increase productivity and food security in our constituency.

healthcare Access

Improving healthcare facilities across Gem to ensure all residents have access to quality and affordable healthcare services, including maternal care, vaccinations, and treatment for common illnesses.
